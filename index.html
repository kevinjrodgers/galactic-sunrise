When humanity is on its final breaths, a band of 7 fearless heroes rise to push back the darkness...will you be able to grasp victory from the depths of despair?

Key Features

  • Rich story: Engross yourself in the world of Galactum with tons of side stories and missions.

  • Explore the solar system: Discover 7 different planets, each filled with unique biomes, characters, and enemies.

  • Intricate turn-based combat: Carefully plan your moves to fight off deadly opponents.

  • Equip and customize your team: With over 100 different weapons and cosmetics, customize your team of heroes to make them truly unique!

  • Learn new skills: Master your characters' abilities to explore the environment and solve challenging puzzles.
